About our website

You can find here all you need to know about our website.


Hi there, I’m Bo and welcome to Video Game Review, your go to source for news and reviews of Games. I started my blogging journey back in 2015 and what started as a way to procrastinate on my school work has grown into a place where I can share my love of game with you!

As a female gamer, it’s my mission to not only spread the gospel of games to everyone I meet, but to use my voice to spotlight games featuring characters and stories that are representative of the wonderfully diverse gaming community. Video Game Review is a place that runs on Girl Magic and Dreams–together I hope that we can create a community of folks that love games and aren’t afraid to share their stories!
